About Me

I am passionate about Computer Graphics, 3D Vision, and Game Design, with a focus on crafting experiences that are both visually striking and technically sophisticated.

My work spans real-time rendering, physically based simulation, and procedural content generation, supported by hands-on experience with C++, Python, OpenGL, Unreal Engine, and Houdini.

I am driven by the idea of bringing digital environments to life - creating worlds that feel authentic, energetic, and emotionally resonant. My long-term goal is to innovate at the intersection of graphics technology and 3D vision.
